1. 'Deep Learning with Differential Privacy': This paper explains how to train deep neural networks with non-convex objectives under a modest privacy budget, using new algorithmic techniques for learning and a refined analysis of privacy costs within the framework of differential privacy.

2. 'The Algorithmic Foundations of Differential Privacy': This paper provides a comprehensive introduction to the problems and techniques of differential privacy, including fundamental techniques for achieving differential privacy and applications of these techniques in creative combinations.

3. 'How to DP-fy ML: A Practical Guide to Machine Learning with Differential Privacy': This paper provides a practical guide to machine learning with differential privacy, focusing on modern ML models that have become more complex, deeper, and harder to reason about.

4. 'Assessing differentially private deep learning with Membership Inference': This paper compares local and central differential privacy mechanisms under white- and black-box membership inference to evaluate their relative privacy-accuracy trade-offs.

5. 'Gradient Leakage Attack Resilient Deep Learning': This paper investigates alternative approaches to gradient leakage resilient deep learning with differential privacy, analyzing existing implementations of deep learning with differential privacy that use fixed noise variance and presenting a new approach that uses dynamic privacy parameters.

6. 'Vision Through the Veil: Differential Privacy in Federated Learning for Medical Image Classification': This paper integrates differential privacy into a federated learning framework for medical image classification, addressing the need for privacy-preserving mechanisms in medical image analysis.
